🧠 The Knowing World

Concept focus: Vertical and horizontal stretches and compressions of the sine graph.

Key ideas to know:

  • The function y = a sin(bx) alters the amplitude and frequency of the base sine wave.
  • Amplitude is controlled by a; frequency (how many cycles appear within 2π) is controlled by b.

Explore the sketch below to observe how changing the values of a and b stretches or compresses the graph.

Prompt: What happens to the graph when you increase or decrease each parameter?
