Worked Solution

➕ Addition

  1. Set-up
    1/2 + 1/3
  2. Transform (Find LCD)
    LCD of 2 and 3 is 6
    1/2 = 3/6, 1/3 = 2/6
  3. Add numerators
    3/6 + 2/6 = 5/6
  4. Simplify
    5/6 is already in lowest terms

➖ Subtraction

  1. Set-up
    1/2 - 1/3
  2. Transform (Find LCD)
    LCD of 2 and 3 is 6
    1/2 = 3/6, 1/3 = 2/6
  3. Subtract numerators
    3/6 - 2/6 = 1/6
  4. Simplify
    1/6 is already in lowest terms

✖ Multiplication

  1. Set-up
    1/2 × 1/3
  2. Transform
    No transformation needed for multiplication
  3. Multiply across
    (1 × 1)/(2 × 3) = 1/6
  4. Simplify
    1/6 is already in lowest terms

➗ Division

  1. Set-up
    1/2 ÷ 1/3
  2. Transform (Keep-Change-Flip)
    1/2 × 3/1
  3. Multiply across
    (1 × 3)/(2 × 1) = 3/2
  4. Simplify
    3/2 = 1½ (mixed number)
