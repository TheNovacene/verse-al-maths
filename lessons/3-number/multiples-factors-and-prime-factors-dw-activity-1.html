How to build your factor tree:

  1. Start with the number at the top
  2. Fill in each pair of blank circles with two numbers that multiply to give the number above them
  3. Continue until you reach prime numbers (which can't be factored further)
  4. Click "Check My Tree" when you're done to see if your factorization is correct
