Sieve of Eratosthenes

Find prime numbers using the ancient sieve method

How to Use the Sieve Method:

  1. Click on the lowest number that has not been crossed out (start with 2)
  2. This will highlight the number as a prime and automatically cross out all its multiples
  3. Click on the next lowest number that has not been crossed out
  4. Continue this process until you've gone through all numbers
  5. The highlighted numbers are all prime numbers!

Tip: Use the "Reset Grid" button to start over, or "Highlight All Primes" to see the answer.
