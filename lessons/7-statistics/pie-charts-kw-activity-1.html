📐 Understanding Pie Chart Angles

Why angles? A full circle = 360°. Each slice's angle shows its share of the whole.

Formula:

Angle = (Frequency ÷ Total) × 360°

Percentages: Each slice also represents a percentage of 100%.

Percentage = (Frequency ÷ Total) × 100%

Key insight: Fraction → Percentage → Angle all represent the same proportion!

  • Percentage: Multiply the fraction by 100 (or fraction × 100)
  • Angle: Multiply the fraction by 360° (or fraction × 360)
