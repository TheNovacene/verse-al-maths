💡 Analysis Tips

  • • Shape: Is the distribution symmetric (balanced), skewed (tail on one side), uniform (flat), or bimodal (two peaks)?
  • • Center: Where are the tallest bars? This shows where most values cluster.
  • • Context: What does the shape reveal about equity, health, consumption, or other SDG themes?
