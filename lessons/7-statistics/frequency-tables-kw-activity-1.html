🔢 Frequency

The number of times a value appears in your data set. If the number 8 appears 3 times, its frequency is 3.

📊 Relative Frequency

Shows what proportion of the total each value represents. Calculated as: Frequency ÷ Total. Can be shown as a decimal (0.25) or percentage (25%).

📈 Cumulative Frequency

A running total that adds up all frequencies up to and including that value. Useful for finding medians and quartiles!
