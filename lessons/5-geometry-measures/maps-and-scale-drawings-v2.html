Map–Scale Design Challenge

THE KNOWING WORLD

Explore how scales represent real-world distances on a map. Use the interactive tool to simulate measurements and translate them into real-world equivalents. Engage with ratios, proportions, and conversion factors.

THE HUMAN STORY

How have cartographers, engineers, and explorers used scale drawings to navigate and design? Why is accuracy crucial in creating scaled representations of our environment? Reflect on historical and modern-day significance.

THE EXPRESSIVE SELF

What personal connections do you have with maps or journeys? Use your creativity to design a scaled map of a place meaningful to you. Think about clarity, labeling, and visual appeal.

THE DESIGNED WORLD

Use the interactive tool to solve challenges involving converting between real and map distances. Calculate unknowns, apply scale factors, and receive instant feedback. What strategies helped you make accurate conversions?

THE LIVING BODY

Pay attention to how your spatial awareness and hand-eye coordination help you visualize and construct scale drawings. Do physical gestures (like measuring with fingers or pointing) aid your understanding?

FINAL TASK

Design a detailed scaled map of a fictional town or environment. Include a clear scale, labeled landmarks, and at least three tasks where the viewer must calculate either real-world or map distances based on your scale.
