📘 Exploring Distance-Time Graphs

Use this interactive app to construct and analyze a journey. Plot the motion on a distance-time graph, then answer related questions to deepen your understanding of speed, intervals, and total distance traveled.

Copy of Journey Builder & Distance-Time Graph Web App Design by Gerry Docherty

🌍 The Knowing World

Use the distance-time graph to interpret speed and changes in motion. What does the slope of each segment represent?

🧭 The Human Story

Imagine this journey is a daily routine. What real-world contexts could this graph represent? Who might experience such a movement?

🎨 The Expressive Self

Create a story to match your graph. Use movement and time creatively to reflect emotional or dramatic events.

🛠️ The Designed World

Distance-time graphs are used in engineering and design to analyze motion, travel efficiency, and system dynamics. How might this apply to transport or robotics?

🧍 The Living Body

How do different rates of motion (like walking, running, or pausing) appear on the graph? What do these intervals say about energy and effort?

✅ Final Task

Design your own journey on the graph. Add stops, changes in speed, and explain what your graph tells us about the trip. Present it to a peer or submit it as a reflection.
