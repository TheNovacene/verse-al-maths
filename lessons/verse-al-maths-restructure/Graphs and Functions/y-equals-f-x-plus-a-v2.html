“What happens when we lift or lower a graph?”

In this lesson, we’ll explore what happens when we shift a graph up or down by adding or subtracting a number to the function:

  • y = f(x) + a — the graph shifts up by a units
  • y = f(x) - a — the graph shifts down by a units

ğŸ§¾ Glossary:

  • Translation: A shift in position (without changing shape)
  • Function: A rule that assigns each input (x) a single output (y)
  • f(x): Function notation, read as “f of x”
  • Vertical shift: Moving a graph up or down
