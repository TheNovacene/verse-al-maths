üìò Glossary
  • Unit Circle: A circle with radius 1 centred at the origin, used to define trig ratios for all angles.
  • CAST Diagram: A mnemonic for remembering which trig ratios are positive in each quadrant.
  • Related Acute Angle: The acute angle formed between the terminal side of an angle and the x-axis.
  • Quadrant: One of four regions divided by the x- and y-axes.
