ğŸ”¢ Introduction to Radians

Before we graph sine waves, we need to understand radian measure — the natural way to measure angles in mathematics.

  • One full circle = 360° = 2π radians
  • π radians = 180° and π/2 radians = 90°
  • Radians are based on the arc length around a unit circle

Imagine wrapping a piece of string around a circle — the angle formed by the arc length is a radian!
