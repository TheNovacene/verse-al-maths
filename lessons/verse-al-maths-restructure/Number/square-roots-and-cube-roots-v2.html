Square roots ask: what number multiplied by itself makes this square? Cube roots ask: what number cubed gives this volume?

Key ideas

  • Square root (√n) finds the side length of a square with area n.
  • Cube root (‚àõn) finds the edge of a cube with volume n.
  • Roots undo powers: if 2² = 4, then √4 = 2; if 3³ = 27, then ‚àõ27 = 3.
