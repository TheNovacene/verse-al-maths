üìå Key Vocabulary:

  • Multiple: A number you get by multiplying another number (e.g., 15 is a multiple of 3)
  • Factor: A number that divides exactly into another number (e.g., 3 is a factor of 15)
  • Prime Factor: A factor that is a prime number
  • LCM: Lowest Common Multiple – smallest multiple two numbers share
  • HCF: Highest Common Factor – largest factor two numbers share
