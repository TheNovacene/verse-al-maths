Focus Concept: Relative Frequency in Probability

Probability is the chance of something happening. Relative frequency is how often an outcome happens compared to the total number of trials, and it gets closer to the true probability the more we repeat the experiment.

Formula:
Relative Frequency = Number of Times Outcome Occurs ÷ Total Number of Trials
