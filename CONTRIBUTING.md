# Contributing to Verse-al Maths

Thank you for being here. This curriculum exists to serve learners — especially those who have been failed by traditional education. Contributions that help us do that better are warmly welcome.

---

## What we welcome

- **Bug fixes** — broken lesson links, rendering issues, typos
- **Accessibility improvements** — anything that makes lessons more usable for neurodivergent learners
- **Metadata enrichment** — improving or completing the JSON metadata files in `/metadata`
- **New lesson HTML files** — following the existing lesson structure and verse-ality framework
- **Scripts and tooling** — utilities that help manage, validate, or build the curriculum
- **Translations** — lessons adapted for other languages or contexts

---

## What to avoid uploading

- Proprietary content from other platforms or publishers
- Student data of any kind
- API keys, tokens, or credentials

---

## Getting started

1. Fork the repo
2. Make your changes on a branch
3. Open a pull request with a clear description of what you changed and why
4. All contributions are reviewed by the Novacene team before merging

---

## Philosophy

This curriculum is built on the belief that learning begins with the learner. If your contribution puts the learner at the centre — their curiosity, their dignity, their way of knowing — it belongs here.
